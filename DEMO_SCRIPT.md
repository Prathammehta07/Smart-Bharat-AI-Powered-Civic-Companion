# Smart Bharat — 2-Minute Demo Pitch Script

This script helps you present the core value proposition and live interactive features of Smart Bharat to judges during a 2-minute pitch window.

---

## 🎙️ Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
- **Presenter:** "Hello judges, today we are pitching **Smart Bharat**, our AI-powered civic companion. The tagline is: *Empowering Citizens. Simplifying Governance.*
- **Problem:** "For millions of Indian citizens, navigating public services and welfare schemes is a labyrinth of legalese, unclear documentation, and delayed grievance tracking."
- **Solution:** "Smart Bharat acts as a friendly digital companion. Let's look at the home dashboard."

---

## 🤖 Feature 1: Conversational AI & Tool Calling (0:30 - 1:00)
- **Action:** Point to the chatbot on the left side of the Home page.
- **Presenter:** "Our conversational interface simplifies bureaucracy. If a citizen wants to apply for a birth certificate, they don\'t need to dig through files. They can click the suggested chip or type it in."
- **Action:** Click the chip: *"How to get a Birth Certificate?"*
- **Presenter:** "Look at how the AI responds. It translates the bureaucratic guidelines into plain, readable steps, lists the document checklist, and provides the direct link. More importantly, notice this banner: the AI automatically executed the `getDocumentChecklist` tool behind the scenes, searching our database."

---

## 📝 Feature 2: Conversational & Form-based Grievance Reporting (1:00 - 1:30)
- **Presenter:** "If a user has a civic issue—like a water leak—they can tell the AI: *'I want to report a water leak opposite Central Park Gate 2'*. Let's type that."
- **Action:** Type *"I want to report a water leak opposite Central Park Gate 2"* and send.
- **Presenter:** "The AI companion triggers the `fileComplaint` tool in the background and lodges a ticket with a Ticket ID. It automatically refreshes our complaints database."
- **Action:** Switch to the **Complaints** tab.
- **Presenter:** "Here is the complaint in our registry! We can view its status and timeline. If we are presenting a demo, we can simulate department response by clicking **Advance State**. Watch the timeline advance from *Submitted* to *Under Review* and *In Progress* instantly."

---

## 🔮 Feature 3: Personalization & Schemes Matching (1:30 - 2:00)
- **Presenter:** "Finally, let's look at **Schemes for You**."
- **Action:** Click the **Schemes for You** tab.
- **Presenter:** "Citizens can fill out a quick demographic questionnaire. Let's select: *Occupation: Farmer, Income: ₹1,20,000, State: Uttar Pradesh* and click match."
- **Action:** Complete the form and submit.
- **Presenter:** "The matching engine reviews welfare rules. It matches us with **PM Kisan Samman Nidhi** and **Ayushman Bharat**, offering a personalized explanation of *why this fits you* along with the document checklists.
- **Presenter (Conclusion):** "Smart Bharat supports 5 languages, making governance inclusive, transparent, and direct. AI for citizens, better governance, and a stronger India. Thank you!"
